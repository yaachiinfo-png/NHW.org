About Us

Reclaiming and reviving lives through worship and the Word.

Our Vision

Reclaiming and reviving lives through the transformative power of praise, worship, and the Word of God, fostering spiritual renewal and growth.

Mission

To create an atmosphere of spiritual awakening where individuals can encounter the living God through praise, worship, and biblical teaching, leading to transformed lives and empowered service.

Our Goal

Our goal is to establish a thriving Christian community that passionately praises God, studies Scripture, and embodies the values of a Christ-centered life. Through biblical education and discipleship, we aim to empower individuals to reflect the character of Christ and serve as exemplary models of faith in our community.

Statement of Faith

We believe in Jesus Christ, the Son of God, and affirm our faith in the supernatural manifestations of His power, including prophecy, healing, and deliverance. Additionally, we believe in the five-fold ministry gifts of Apostles, Prophets, Evangelists, Pastors, and Teachers, as outlined in Ephesians 4:11, to equip and build up the Body of Christ.
